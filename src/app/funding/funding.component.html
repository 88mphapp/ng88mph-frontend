Community Grants

Community grants aim at encouraging and developing the 88mph community and 88mph itself. Those grants will be received by users, content creators and projects supporting 88mph. Those users may have helped out users, contributed in a meaningful way to the community, created content, guides, NFTs, etc. Grants range from $100 to $2,000 to be paid in MPH.

Users or applications with questions may join this  Telegram or the Grants channel on  88mph Discord.
