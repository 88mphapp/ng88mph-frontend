How it works

Learn how 88mph generates fixed APY and stays solvent.

Fixed APY

88mph determines its fixed-interest rate according to the deposit's duration by giving between 25% and 75% of a 30-day EMA pulled from the variable APY of lending protocols.

Yield generation

88mph pools deposits and lends them at a variable APY on protocols like Aave, Yearn, and Compound.

Insolvency risk outsourcing

88mph's yield tokens allow investors, willing to speculate on the future variable APY of Compound or Aave, to fund the fixed APY that 88mph owes to its users. In exchange, they earn the variable APY generated by the users' deposits and the fixed APY funded.

Dynamic pools surplus rebalancing

The variable APY generated by your deposit can lead to a surplus of yield compared to what 88mph owes you; by consequence, improving the solvency for every pool via a dynamic pools surplus rebalancing.
