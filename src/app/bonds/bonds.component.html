Speculate on future yields

Yield tokens (YT) allow speculators to profit from the rise in the variable yield rate of lending protocols or hedge part of their borrowing costs of a loan. YT are available when a fixed yield rate deposit is made on 88mph, as each YT is tied to a deposit. YT give holders the right to earn all the future variable-rate yields generated by the corresponding deposit + the purchase cost of the YTs. Learn more .
