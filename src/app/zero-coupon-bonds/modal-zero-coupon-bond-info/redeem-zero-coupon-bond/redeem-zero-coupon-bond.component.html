How it works

When the zero coupon bond becomes mature, you can redeem your zero coupon bond 1-to-1 for {{poolInfo.stablecoinSymbol}}.

However, it's likely that you won't be able to do so immediately after maturation, because the individual deposits need to be withdrawn first in order to provide the {{poolInfo.stablecoinSymbol}}. The maximum redeemable amount is displayed below. The owners of the deposits are incentivized to withdraw the deposits, since they will receive their MPH reward after withdrawal, so feel free to wait until enough deposits have been withdrawn to redeem your zero coupon bonds.

If you urgently need to redeem your bonds for some reason, you also have the option to go to the "Withdraw MPH reward from deposit" tab and withdraw the deposits yourself.
